CONVERTIBLE SPACE, ENDLESS FUN

Solid Ground Arena is a multi-sport indoor arena and the new home of Arizona Inline Hockey. We have a full-size 185 x 80 indoor roller hockey rink that we use for Arizona inline hockey, roller derby and roller skating.

We offer youth leagues on Saturday mornings and adult leagues every day of the week.

Our indoor rink is also convertible into 4 full-size competition volleyball courts, and we offer daily, weekly, or monthly volleyball rentals. We also have a 110 x 42 multi-purpose court for various sports and activities.

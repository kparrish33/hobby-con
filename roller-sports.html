Registration

This page is to register for upcoming recreational inline hockey leagues, upcoming inline hockey tournaments, drop in or pickup games or classes and clinics.

To register online for one of our Arizona inline hockey leagues please click the league you are interested in joining. The league link will take you to the league page with schedules, rosters and statistics. In the top right corner of every roller hockey league page is a link to the online registration for that specific roller hockey league. Once on the registration page follow the page filling in all the boxes marked with a red asterisk.

Once the inline hockey league has started you will need to go on the league listing page to see our current leagues in progress. You can follow the same steps by clicking on the league link.

We thank you for your interest in playing roller hockey in Arizona at Solid Ground Arena. If you need additional help feel free to email us or text us at 623-878-2444. You are welcome to stop by and check out our leagues any night of the week. Our staff will be happy to assist you in finding the right league to join.

Leagues

First, our D1 roller hockey league is our top league for advanced Arizona roller hockey players. Our D1 league currently plays games on Sunday evening. Therefore it is recommended for travel players seeking the highest level of roller hockey in Arizona.

Secondly, the D2 roller hockey league is an advanced/intermediate level of play. Our D2 league currently plays all their games on Thursday evenings. Therefore intermediate and advanced players will compete in a highly skilled league. D3 is an intermediate league with a mixed skill level of experienced intermediate players.

Thirdly, our D4 and D5 leagues are both novice levels of play for players with some experience and moderate skill. Sunday and Tuesday D5 leagues are for beginner league for novice players.

The Solid Ground Arena management team reserves the option to move teams up or down a skill level to balance the leagues.

Coed divisions roller hockey leagues may be split into two skill levels depending on the amount of teams that register. Coed is a novice skill level. Family and Friends is a higher skill level. All our Coed leagues carry a two goal per player limit and no slap shots.

Wednesday Over 30 roller hockey league is an intermediate level. Wednesday Masters is a skilled league with a slower pace. The Masters roller hockey league is for the most experienced players over 45 but who have lost some of their speed but not their skill over the years... average age in Masters is 52 years old. Monday Over 40 is a mix of experienced players. We split our Monday over 40 league into a quicker pace called Over 40 and a slower pace called Over 45. Both leagues are competitive skill levels.

Rules & Regulations

Solid Ground Arena is a member club of the AAU (Amateur Athletic Union) and USARS. USARS (USA Roller Sports) is the National Governing Body of roller hockey in the United States designated by the IOC (International Olympic Committee) and USOPC (United States Olympic Committee). Solid Ground Arena follows the rules and guidelines in the World Skate Rulebook.

Solid Ground Arena is a multi-sport indoor arena and the new home of Arizona Inline Hockey. We have a full-size 185 x 80 indoor roller hockey rink that we use for Arizona inline hockey, roller derby and roller skating.

We offer youth leagues on Saturday mornings and adult leagues every day of the week.

Our indoor rink is also convertible into 4 full-size competition volleyball courts, and we offer daily, weekly, or monthly volleyball rentals. We also have a 110 x 42 multi-purpose court for various sports and activities.
